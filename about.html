About Us

Discover our mission, history, and what makes The Weatherhead Fund unique.

Our Mission

Developing Exceptional Investors

The Weatherhead Fund is a student-run investment organization at Case Western Reserve University's Weatherhead School of Management. We manage a $1.3 million long-only public equities portfolio using fundamental investing principles.

Our mission is twofold: to provide members with hands-on experience in equity research and portfolio management, and to prepare them for successful careers in investment banking, private equity, and asset management.

Each semester, analysts from our six sector verticals conduct deep-dive research and pitch one stock for potential inclusion in the portfolio. This rigorous process mirrors the work done at professional investment firms and provides invaluable experience for our members.

Our History

Since 2011

From humble beginnings to managing over a million dollars in assets.
