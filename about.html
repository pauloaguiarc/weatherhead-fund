About The Fund

The Weatherhead Fund is a student-run investment fund at Case Western Reserve University managing a $1.3M long-only public equities portfolio. We combine hands-on portfolio management with comprehensive professional development to prepare our members for careers in finance.

Our Mission

To empower students to become exceptional investors and finance professionals through hands-on experience and rigorous training in fundamental equity research and portfolio management.
