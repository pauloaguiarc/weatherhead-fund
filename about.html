About Us

History & Mission

The Weatherhead Fund is Case Western Reserve University's student-run investment organization, where members develop as investors through hands-on equity research, stock pitching, and active portfolio discussion.

Our mission is to provide an authentic, practical investing experience while cultivating curiosity, analytical rigor, and long-term learning. We train students from the ground up and support their growth through mentorship, leadership, and exposure to industry professionals.

Organization

Analysts are placed into sector-focused verticals where they collaborate in small teams to source, evaluate, and debate investment ideas. Each vertical is guided by experienced student leaders and contributes research to the broader investment process.

Across the fund, members build technical and communication skills by performing fundamental analysis, creating valuation work, and presenting recommendations to peers and leadership.

Process

Admission is selective and focused on motivated underclassmen who demonstrate strong curiosity, coachability, and commitment. We do not require prior finance experience.

New members begin with a structured education track that covers accounting, valuation, and markets. Following training, members transition into analyst roles where they apply these tools through live company research and formal pitch discussions.
